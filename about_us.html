About Us

Bhartiya Yuva Computer Saksharata Mission is an autonomous institution registered under State & Central Govt.
BYCSM is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Computer Education Institute. BYCSM Logo is Register under Govt. of India.
BYCSM is a leading Computer Education Network in all over India. The Organization has its spread in 11 States of the country.
The main objective behind the establishment of this institute is to spread computer literacy to all over the country.
Through this computer Literacy program, we ensure that students may learn more and more become self-motivated.
